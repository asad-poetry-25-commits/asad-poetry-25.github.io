Lafz bolte hain, par dil khamosh hai...

"Farsh par bikhre dil ka ek ek tukda,
meri ek ek shayari hai." 💔

Mashoor hun us dard ke liye jo mujhe virasat mein mila...🥀

— Asad Azam

Yahan meri 1000+ dard bhari shayari ka silsila jald shuru hoga.
